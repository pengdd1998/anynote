AnyNote is a local-first, privacy-first note-taking app with true end-to-end encryption. Your notes are encrypted on your device before they ever leave it -- the server never sees your plaintext content.

ZERO-KNOWLEDGE ARCHITECTURE
AnyNote uses XChaCha20-Poly1305 authenticated encryption with keys derived via Argon2id and HKDF-SHA256. Every note gets its own unique encryption key. The server stores only encrypted blobs and cannot read your data.

WORKS OFFLINE
Your notes live on your device first. Create, edit, and organize without an internet connection. Sync automatically when you are back online.

RICH EDITOR
Write with a full-featured rich text editor supporting markdown, code blocks, math formulas, and image attachments. Use keyboard shortcuts for efficient writing on desktop.

CROSS-DEVICE SYNC
Encrypted sync across all your devices -- iPhone, iPad, Mac, Windows, Android, and web. Conflict resolution with version vectors ensures nothing is lost.

AI-POWERED FEATURES (OPTIONAL)
Use your own LLM API key or the shared server model for AI-assisted writing. AI requests are proxied directly -- we never log request or response content.

PUBLISH ANYWHERE
Publish your notes directly to Xiaohongshu, Medium, WordPress, and more. Platform credentials are encrypted at rest.

ORGANIZE YOUR WAY
Tags, collections, and full-text search keep your notes organized. FTS5-powered search handles both English and Chinese text effortlessly.

OPEN SOURCE
AnyNote is open source. Audit the code, self-host your own instance, or contribute to the project.

Your notes. Your privacy. No compromises.
